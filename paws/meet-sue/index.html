Meet Sue

A different approach to dog grooming...

Sue is an experienced dog owner and groomer in Nottingham who's putting animal care and service first.

Many dog owners report a less-than-satisfactory service from their current dog groomer or chain pet shop. Sue looks to offer something missing from the business of dog grooming; personal service, informed advice from experience, and an authentic care for your dog that's clear to see. Sue aims to provide an experience that both you and your dog will love, and a cut to your requirements.

A mother of four children, as well as being a long-time animal lover and dog owner of 30 years, Sue holds a degree in Science and a City & Guilds ation in Dog Grooming backed up with years of experience grooming (and owning!) many types of dogs.

What you can expect:

  • Flexible appointments; able to fit around your schedule
  • Always a friendly, no-fuss service
  • Impartial advice on coat care
  • Extra patience for difficult or nervous dogs
  • De-matting, wash, dry and fur treatments available

Making a booking

Places can be limited especially around Christmas, so please book early using the form, or send a message via Facebook. We look forward to hearing from you.
